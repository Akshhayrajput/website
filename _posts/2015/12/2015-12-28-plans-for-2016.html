What a difference twelve months can make. A year ago, I was wrapping up my second month without a salary and had serious doubts about whether Software Carpentry was going to be viable. Today, our new Executive Director is in place, our Program Coordinator has our workshops running more smoothly than ever, our workshop administration tool is ticking over nicely, we're about to elect our second Steering Committee, we've published our lessons, we have 18 partners and affiliates, and our sibling organization, Data Carpentry, has received a major grant that will keep it running for at least three years.
